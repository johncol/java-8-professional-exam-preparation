Wristbands

When you are admitted to a hospital, they place on your wrist a white wristband with your name on it. But there are
other different colored wristbands which symbolize other things. The red wristbands are placed on dead people.

There was one surgeon who worked on night shift in a school hospital. He had just finished an operation and was on
his way down to the basement. He entered the elevator and there was just one other person there...
